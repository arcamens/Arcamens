Kanban Methodology

With arcamens you can apply kanban strategy in a quite powerful manner. Arcamens boards implement a different and powerful manner of visualizing kanban boards.

Scrum Methodology

With arcamens boards and timelines you can apply scrum strategy in many different ways. Ideas, suggestions, user stories can go over timelines where features can be prioritized.

Timelines

Timelines can play several roles when designing a business workflow. When mixed up with arcamens's boards it is a powerful tool for optmizing the workings of a business. Timelines's posts can be used to delegate tasks, notify events, sharing information or social interaction. Not all business demand the usage of boards for task delegation thus timelines can be used to integrate different kind of sectors. Board cards can be forked into timeline posts it allows integrating all sectors of your business painlessly no matter if you own a small or a large business, timelines will be a powerful tool.

Boards

Arcamens's boards are dispalyed in a new and powerful manner. It gives the users a perfect perspective of lists and cards. It is simple to move, copy cards and lists around boards, You can fork a card into multiple cards/posts, these subtasks can be over other boards, such a mechanism allows an ideal approach for task delegation between multiple sectors of a business.

Tags

Arcamens provides tagging mechanisms for timelines's posts and boards's cards as well as user tagging. It allows a high level of organization and a powerful search tool.

Event System

Arcamens has a powerful event system, whatever it happens everyone will be notified of. It allows managers to easily keep track of what employees are doing and how them are doing as well giving feedback quickly. Every worker action generates an event, such events can be commented out by you. It makes all kind of worker's actions to be criticized in an organized manner thus improving your workers's performance and work quality.

Mobile

Arcamens UI was carefully designed to be intuitive and highly flexible it works seamlessly over mobiles with suble differences.

Organizations

Arcamens allows multiple organizations attached to a given account. You can have as many organizations as you want regardless of the plan you pay for. You can easily integrate all your business's branches effortless.
