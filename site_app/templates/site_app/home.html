Abstract to divide, divide to rule!

Ideal for all teams whose main goals are flexibility and productivity.

Kanban Methodology

With arcamens you can apply kanban strategy in a quite powerful manner. Arcamens boards implement a different and powerful manner of visualizing kanban boards.

Scrum Methodology

With arcamens boards and groups you can apply scrum methodology in many different ways. Ideas, suggestions, user stories can go over groups where features can be prioritized or forked into kanban board cards.

Groups

Groups can play several roles when designing a business workflow. When mixed up with arcamens's boards it is a powerful tool for optmizing the workings of a business. Groups's posts can be used to delegate tasks, notify events, sharing knowledge or social interaction. Not all business demand the usage of boards for task delegation thus groups can be used to integrate different kind of a business sectors.

Boards

Arcamens's boards are dispalyed in a new and powerful manner. It gives the users a perfect perspective of lists and cards. It is simple to move, copy cards and lists around boards, You can fork a card into multiple cards. You can assign a given task/card to people who are outside of its board.

Tags

Arcamens provides tagging mechanisms for groups's posts and boards's cards as well as user tagging. It allows a high level of organization and a powerful search tool.

Event System

Arcamens has a powerful event system, whatever it happens everyone will be notified of. It allows managers to easily keep track of what employees are doing and how them are doing as well giving feedback quickly. Every worker action generates an event, such events can be commented out by you. It makes all kind of worker's actions to be criticized in an organized manner thus improving your workers's performance and work quality. It implements notification using web pushes which allows you to always be aware of what is going on.

Mobile

Arcamens UI was carefully designed to be intuitive and highly flexible it works seamlessly over mobiles with suble differences.

Organizations

Arcamens allows multiple organizations attached to a given account. You can have as many organizations as you want regardless of the plan you pay for. You can easily integrate all your business's branches effortlessly.

Software Development

Arcamens integrates with Bitbucket and Github, you can relate cards and commits easily.

Workspaces

Workers have their own workspace in the organization. A worker can create as many boards/groups as necessary and share content easily with a concrete strategy for avoiding security leaks.

Powerful Searches

Arcamens allows with no doubt the most powerful search mechanism. It is possible to search for cards/posts based on notes, snippets, owner, workers, attachments, created time using a fancy and flexible filter language.
