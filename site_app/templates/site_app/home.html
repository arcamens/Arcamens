Kanban methodology

With arcamens you can apply kanban strategy easily due to its new concept of kanban boards, arcamens boards are quite a different and powerful manner of doing kanban.

Scrum methodology

With arcamens boards and timelines you can apply scrum strategy in many different ways thus achieving high flexiblity which entails better productivity.

Timelines

Timelines can play several roles when designing a business workflow. When mixed up with arcamens's boards it is a powerful tool for optmizing the workings of a business. Timelines's posts can be used to delegate tasks, notify events, sharing information etc.

Boards

Arcamens's boards are dispalyed in a new and much more powerful manner. It gives the users a perfect perspective of lists and cards. It is simple to move, copy cards and lists around boards and organizations.

Tags

Arcamens provides tagging mechanisms for timelines's posts and boards's cards as well as user tagging. It allows a high level of organization and a powerful search tool.

Event System

Arcamens has a powerful event system, whatever it happens everyone will be notified of. It allows managers to easily keep track of what employees are doing and how them are doing.
